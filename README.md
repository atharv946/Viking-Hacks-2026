OVERVIEW OF THIS PROJECT

Currently, artists face problems such as the lack of feedback during their creation phase. It is also expensive to get professional feedback on uncompleted art. This is why we developed Redline. 

Redline assists artists by providing them feedback and filters on their uncompleted art pieces. We developed a total of 6 features that artists can use. Here is the list:

Critique feature which gives general feedback on the art by analyzing the art
Relight feature which allows artists to see how their art would look in different light settings. 
Layers feature allows artists to move around the layers and see the layers in their piece
Frames feature lets you make sure that you are putting features in one focus box
Style DNA feature tells all of the main colors in the drawing so that you know what colors to use
Readability feature
e shows the areas that are the most readable and are instantly seen by the viewer
